Where It Goes

Your Dollar in Action

Every contribution goes directly toward putting boys in gyms across Nebraska. Here's what we fund:

🏟️

Gym Time

Court rental is our biggest cost — every clinic needs a welcoming gym and enough space for players to learn.

🏐

Equipment

Volleyballs, pinnies, nets. The gear that makes programming possible.

🛡️

Insurance

Participant accident coverage and general liability statewide.

📣

Outreach

Getting the word out in communities across Nebraska, especially underserved areas.

Grassroots Giving

Give What Fits

This is built one family, one coach, and one community supporter at a time. Small gifts help keep the door open for more boys to try volleyball.

First Serve
$10

Helps with the basics: balls, printing, check-in supplies, and the little things that make a clinic work.

Community Boost
$25

Helps keep clinic fees low so families can say yes when the next opportunity opens.

Gym Helper
$100

Goes toward court time, equipment, and insurance for beginner-friendly programming.

Founding Backer
$250

A top-level grassroots gift that helps launch more affordable boys volleyball opportunities across Nebraska.
