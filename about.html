Why Nebraska
Boys Volleyball Exists

It started with a kid at an open gym in Iowa. It became a question we couldn't stop asking.

The Founding Story

One Kid. One Question.

A couple years ago, a trip to a volleyball clinic in Iowa turned into the conversation that started this work. Sitting with other parents, we heard about communities where boys volleyball was already growing — not because everything was perfectly organized, but because people kept opening doors for kids to walk through.

One Minnesota dad said something simple that stuck: "Sometimes it only takes one parent."

Then he pointed to a boy on the court. The boy had not really found his place yet. Another kid had been inviting boys to open gym on Friday nights, trying to grow the game and build a community around it. This boy planned to stop by for a few minutes and then go to a party. He stayed the whole night. Then came back the next Friday. Then Sunday. Eventually, he was showing up every chance he got.

Volleyball gave him more than a sport. It gave him a place to be, people who expected to see him again, and a community he could be part of.

That story followed us home to Nebraska. It became the question behind Nebraska Boys Volleyball: how many boys here are in the same spot, just waiting for someone to open a door?

Nebraska Boys Volleyball was built by people who believe that door should exist in more towns, for more families, and for any boy willing to show up and try. None of us had every answer at the beginning. We just knew the opportunity mattered, and we knew it would take a community to build it.

— Nebraska Boys Volleyball Founding Team

Who We Are

A Nonprofit Built to Remove Barriers

Nebraska Boys Volleyball is the trade name of Nebraska Volleyball Development Association, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit incorporated in Nebraska. Our mission is simple: get more boys into volleyball, regardless of where they live or what their family can afford.

We are not a club. We don't compete with club programs. Nebraska Boys Volleyball is an additive recreational development program. We exist for the kids who haven't found their sport yet — or who never got the invitation.

We are building more than clinics. We are building entry points into a volleyball community for boys who may not know the sport is for them yet.

🎯

Mission

Expand access to boys volleyball statewide through affordable, community-based programming.

📜

501(c)(3) Nonprofit

Nebraska Volleyball Development Association. Donations are tax-deductible. We answer to our community, not a profit motive.

🤝

Independent

Nebraska Boys Volleyball is not affiliated with any club or regional governing body. We operate on our own terms with our community's interests first.

How We Operate

A Few Things We Believe

Cost Should Not Be the Barrier

Programming is designed to stay low-cost and accessible. Families who can contribute are encouraged to, and scholarship support may be available when possible.

No Tryouts

No rosters. No cuts. You show up, you play. Every time.

Come as You Are

Beginner, returning player, tall kid who's never tried — all welcome. The only requirement is showing up.

Local Roots

Nebraska Boys Volleyball chapters carry their city's name and are built around local relationships, local gyms, and local community leaders.

Want to Be Part of It?

Whether you're a player, parent, coach, or just someone who wants to help — there's a place for you in Nebraska Boys Volleyball.
