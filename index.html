Why We Exist

It Started With One Player

"I was at a clinic in Iowa when a dad from Minnesota pointed out a boy on the court. The kid had almost skipped open gym to go to a party, but someone kept inviting him back. He stayed all night. Then he came back the next Friday. Then every chance he could."

"I never learned his name. But driving home, I kept thinking about how many boys in Nebraska might be in the same spot: interested, unsure, and waiting for someone to open the door."

That's why Nebraska Boys Volleyball exists. Not to build another club. To make sure more boys know there is a place for them to try.

What We Do

Affordable Programming. Fewer Barriers.

Nebraska Boys Volleyball runs a rotating clinic series across Nebraska with two development tracks. Whether it's someone's first time touching a volleyball or they've been playing for years, there's a spot for them.

🏐

Junior Development

7th grade and under. Small-sided games, skill fundamentals, ~90 minutes. Built for beginners and first-timers.

🔥

Senior Development

8th grade and above. Full 6v6, standard format, competitive reps. Come ready to play.

📍

Community Growth

We build programming around local relationships, gym partners, and communities ready to create more opportunities for boys.
