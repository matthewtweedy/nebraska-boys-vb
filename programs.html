Programming

What We Run

Low-cost clinics and open gyms built to be welcoming, affordable, and beginner-friendly.

Weekly Clinics

Two Tracks. One Goal.

Every Nebraska Boys Volleyball clinic runs two tracks simultaneously so players of different ages and experience levels all get the right kind of reps. Show up, check in, and we'll get you in the right group.

🟡

Junior Development

7th grade and under

Small-sided games, skill fundamentals, beginner-friendly format. About 90 minutes. Perfect for first-timers and newer players.

🔴

Senior Development

8th grade and above

Full 6v6, standard format, competitive reps. Come ready to play hard.

No tryouts. No rosters. No experience required. Register for the next clinic in your area and we'll take care of the rest. Programming is designed to stay affordable and welcoming.
